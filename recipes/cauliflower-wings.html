Cauliflower Wings

Description

A crispy, baked delight. Ready to be spun in your favorite sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup of 1:1 baking flour (can substitute all-purpose)
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp of crushed red pepper flakes(optional)
  • 1 cup of panko breading

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Line baking sheet with greased foil
  2. Remove florets from cauliflower. Chop into smaller pieces, roughly bite-size.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, milk, salt, black pepper, garlic powder and red pepper flakes together.
  4. Put panko breadcrumbs in a seperate bowl. Place next to the bowl with the batter.
  5. Toss the cauliflower one at a time. Shake off excess batter. Dip cauliflower in the breadcrumbs to coat, and place on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 22 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and toss in your favorite sauce.
