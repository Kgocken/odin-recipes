Spinach Lasagna

Description

A tasty vegetarian lasagna with plenty of cheese and spinach to satisfy the whole family

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 24oz of fresh spinach
  • 15oz of ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp of salt, more or less to taste
  • 1/4 tsp of pepper, more or less to taste
  • 1/2 tsp of italian seasoning

Assembly

  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 24oz jar of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • cooking spray
  • 2 Tbs of chopped parsley

Instructions

For the filling

  1. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium high heat. Add as much spinach as will fit in the pan and cook it until it wilts. Continue adding spinach to the pan until it's all wilted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Let the spinach cool a bit, then squeeze the excess water out. Coarsely chop the spinach
  3. Combine the spinach, ricotta, eggs, parmesan, 1 cup of mozzerella, salt, pepper and italian seasoning in a large bowl. Stir to mix.

For assembly

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit. Coat a 9"x13" pan with cooking spray.
  3. Spread a small amount of sauce on the bottom of the pan. Add a layer of noodles, then spread 1/3 of the filling mixture over the noodles.
  4. Top the filling with 1/3 of the remaining marinara.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 ending with marinara sauce
  6. Cover the pan with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  7. Uncover and top with 2 cups of mozzarella. Bake an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and browned.
  8. Sprinkle lasagna with parsley. Let stand 5 or 10 minutes, then cut and serve.
